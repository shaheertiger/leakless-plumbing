Know the Signs

Warning Signs of a Water Leak

Leaks rarely announce themselves. If you notice any of these around your GTA home, don't wait — a quick call helps us catch it early.

Rising Water Bills

A sudden or steady climb in your water bill with no change in habits is one of the clearest signs of a hidden leak somewhere in the system.

Damp Spots & Stains

Discoloured patches, bubbling paint, or soft drywall on walls and ceilings mean water is escaping where it shouldn't.

Musty Smell

A persistent damp, earthy odour — especially in a basement, closet, or under a sink — often points to moisture from a slow leak.

Mold Growth

Black, green, or fuzzy patches appearing on walls, grout, or baseboards are a red flag that a leak is feeding constant moisture.

Dripping or Running Sounds

Hearing water trickle or run when every tap is off is a strong signal that a pipe is leaking behind a wall or under the floor.

Warped Floors & Low Pressure

Buckling floorboards, lifting tiles, or a sudden drop in water pressure can all trace back to a leak quietly working behind the scenes.

How do I know if I have a hidden water leak?

Common signs of a hidden water leak include an unexplained jump in your water bill, damp spots or stains on walls and ceilings, a musty smell, the sound of running or dripping water when everything is off, or low water pressure. The fastest way to know for sure is to call 437-996-1996 and describe what you're seeing so a GTA plumber can help you check.

Yes. We locate leaks hidden inside walls, ceilings, and floors so we can open only what's necessary and repair the source. Call 437-996-1996 and tell us where you see staining, bubbling paint, or drips, and we'll track the leak down and fix it.

What are signs of a slab leak?

Signs of a slab or foundation leak include warm or damp spots on the floor, unexplained cracks, a spike in your water bill, low pressure, or the sound of water running under the floor. These leaks can undermine your foundation, so call 437-996-1996 right away to have it located and repaired.
