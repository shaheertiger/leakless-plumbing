Warning Signs

Signs Your Sump Pump Is Failing

A sump pump usually warns you before it quits. If you notice any of these signs, call 437-256-9440 and have a GTA plumber check it out before the next heavy rain.

Strange Noises

Rattling, grinding, or gurgling from the pit often points to a worn impeller, a jammed motor, or debris in the system.

Constant Running

A pump that never shuts off is overworked and heading for early failure — usually a switch, sizing, or pit problem.

Not Turning On

If water is rising in the pit but the pump stays silent, the float switch, wiring, or motor may have failed. Call now.

Visible Rust or Corrosion

Rust on the pump or corrosion in the pit signals age and wear — and can clog the system with iron deposits.

Frequent Cycling

Turning on and off every few seconds wears the motor out fast and often means a switch or check-valve issue.

Seven Years or Older

Most pumps last 7 to 10 years. If yours is past that mark, it is living on borrowed time — plan a replacement now.

Water in the Basement

Damp spots, puddles, or a musty smell mean water is getting past your pump. Do not wait — call a GTA plumber today.

Sump Pump Questions, Answered

Common questions from GTA homeowners about sump pumps. Have another? Call 437-256-9440 and a local plumber will answer it.

How long does a sump pump last?

Most sump pumps last about 7 to 10 years, though hard-working pumps in high-water-table GTA basements can wear out sooner. If yours is getting older or running more often, call 437-256-9440 and a local plumber will assess it over the phone and, if needed, schedule a replacement before the next big storm.

What is a backup sump pump?

A backup sump pump is a second pump — usually battery-powered — that takes over if your main pump fails or the power goes out during a storm. It is one of the best ways to protect a GTA basement from flooding. Call 437-256-9440 and we will recommend the right backup setup for your home.

My sump pump is not working — what should I do?

If your sump pump is not turning on and water is rising, call 437-256-9440 right away and a GTA plumber can walk you through what to check and get out to you fast. A dead pump during heavy rain can flood a basement within hours, so it is best to call the moment you notice a problem.

Do I need a sump pump in the GTA?

Many GTA homes need a sump pump because of the region's high water table, spring melt, and heavy summer storms — especially homes with finished basements or a history of water problems. Call 437-256-9440 and we will tell you whether a sump pump makes sense for your property.

Can you install a battery backup sump pump?

Yes. We install battery backup sump pumps throughout Toronto and the GTA so your basement stays protected even during a power outage. Call 437-256-9440 and we will price a battery backup system for your basement over the phone.
