Stay Calm — Here's the Plan

What to Do Right Now

A few quick actions can limit the damage before we arrive. Follow these steps, and remember: the fastest thing you can do is pick up the phone.

1

Shut Off the Water

Turn off the nearest isolation valve, or shut down your home's main water valve (usually in the basement near where the line enters). This alone can stop a small emergency from becoming a big one.

2

Protect Valuables

Move furniture, electronics, boxes, and anything valuable away from the water. If it's safe to do so, switch off power to the affected area to avoid electrical hazards.

3

Call 437-256-9440

Call us right away. A real local plumber answers 24/7 — no forms, no waiting on a callback. Tell us what's happening and where you are in the GTA, and we'll get moving.

4

Wait for Guidance

Stay on the line if you'd like. We'll give you clear, calm instructions for your exact situation and keep you posted while our plumber heads your way.
