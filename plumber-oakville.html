Plumbing That Fits Oakville's Housing

Oakville's plumbing needs vary a lot by neighbourhood. Character homes near the lake in Old Oakville and Bronte often still have original galvanized supply lines or aging cast iron drains that need careful diagnosis rather than a guess-and-replace approach.
