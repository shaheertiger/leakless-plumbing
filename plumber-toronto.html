Local Knowledge

Plumbing That Fits Toronto's Housing

Toronto's plumbing challenges depend a lot on the neighbourhood. Century homes in Cabbagetown, The Annex and Riverdale often still have original cast iron drain lines or clay sewer laterals that corrode and narrow with age. Downtown and midtown high-rises bring building-management access rules and shared stack plumbing. Newer construction in the inner suburbs comes with its own modern fixtures and code requirements.

We work across all of it — houses, condos, semis, and commercial units — and we diagnose before we recommend, so you are not paying to replace something that just needs a proper repair.
