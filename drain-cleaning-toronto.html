Why Toronto Drains Clog

Older Toronto Pipe, Explained

A lot of Toronto's recurring drain problems trace back to the city's age. Century homes in The Annex, Cabbagetown, Riverdale, and The Beaches often still have original cast iron drain lines or clay sewer laterals — pipe that corrodes, narrows, and cracks with decades of use, and is far more prone to tree root intrusion than modern PVC.

We clear these lines properly and use camera inspection to confirm the real condition of your pipe, so you're not paying to clear the same blockage twice. If sewage is backing up into your basement or lowest fixtures, that's an emergency — see our Toronto emergency plumber page and call us immediately.

Toronto Drain Cleaning FAQ

Clogged Drain Questions, Answered

Have a drain question that isn't here? The fastest answer is always a quick call to 437-996-1996.

Why do drains clog so often in older Toronto homes?

Many century homes in Toronto's older neighbourhoods still run on original cast iron drain lines or clay sewer laterals. Decades of grease, root intrusion, and pipe scale narrow these lines, so they clog more often than modern plastic pipe. Call 437-996-1996 and we'll clear the clog and tell you honestly whether the pipe itself needs attention.

Can you clear a clogged drain in a downtown Toronto condo?

Yes. We regularly clear in-suite drains in downtown and midtown Toronto condo towers, and we coordinate with property management for building access when needed. Call 437-996-1996 to book a visit.

Do you use a camera to inspect Toronto sewer lines?

When a drain keeps clogging or a sewer line is involved, we use a waterproof drain camera to see exactly what's inside the pipe — roots, grease, cracks, or a bellied section common in older Toronto infrastructure. Call 437-996-1996 to book a camera drain inspection.

How fast can you clear a clogged drain in Toronto?

Most single clogged drains are cleared in one visit, often same day, anywhere in the city. Call 437-996-1996, describe the problem, and we'll give you a straight answer on timing for your neighbourhood.

Do you handle grease clogs for Toronto restaurants?

Absolutely. Grease-clogged kitchen lines and grease traps are one of the most common commercial calls we get from Toronto restaurants and cafés. We clear built-up grease and keep your kitchen running with minimal downtime. Call 437-996-1996.
