Sewer Camera Inspection FAQ

Camera Inspection Questions, Answered

Have a question that is not here? The fastest answer is always a quick call to 437-996-1996.

What is a sewer camera inspection and how does it work?

A sewer camera inspection uses a waterproof camera head mounted on a flexible cable that we feed directly into your drain or sewer line. The camera transmits live video to a monitor as it travels through the pipe, letting us see cracks, roots, blockages, bellies, and pipe material firsthand instead of guessing. Call 437-996-1996 to book a camera inspection anywhere in the GTA.

Should I get a sewer camera inspection before buying a home?

Yes, especially for older homes in Toronto and across the GTA that may still have original clay or cast iron sewer laterals. Those lines can be cracked, root-infiltrated, or bellied with no visible symptoms yet, and a camera inspection before closing can reveal a costly problem while you still have room to negotiate. Call 437-996-1996 to schedule a pre-purchase inspection.

Can a camera inspection find tree root intrusion?

Yes. Tree roots entering through pipe joints, cracks, or old clay tile connections show up clearly on camera, and we can tell you roughly how far down the line the intrusion is and how severe it is. That lets us recommend the right fix, whether it is clearing the roots or a more permanent repair. Call 437-996-1996 if you suspect root intrusion.

Do you provide a recording or report of the inspection?

Yes. We record every camera inspection and walk you through what we found in plain language while we are still on site, so you have a clear picture of your pipe's condition for your own records, for repair planning, or for a real estate transaction. Call 437-996-1996 for details on what is included with your inspection.

How fast can you schedule a camera inspection?

Many camera inspections across the GTA can be booked same day or the next day, depending on your location and our current schedule. Call 437-996-1996, tell us what is going on, and we will give you a straight answer on the soonest available appointment.
