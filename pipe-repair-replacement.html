FAQ

Pipe Repair & Replacement Questions

Quick answers. When in doubt, call 437-996-1996.

How do I know if my pipes need to be replaced instead of repaired?

Frequent leaks, discoloured water, low water pressure, or visible corrosion are all signs your pipes may need replacing rather than another spot repair. Call 437-996-1996 and we'll inspect the system and give you an honest recommendation.

Do you replace poly-B and galvanized steel pipes?

Yes. Poly-B and galvanized steel pipes were common in GTA homes for decades and are prone to failure as they age. We repipe with modern copper or PEX to eliminate the ongoing risk.

What should I do about a frozen or burst pipe?

Shut off your water main if you can safely reach it, then call 437-996-1996 right away. Frozen and burst pipes are urgent — we treat these as emergency calls and get a plumber moving fast.

How much does repiping a house cost?

Cost depends on the size of your home, the pipe material, and how accessible the existing plumbing is. Call 437-996-1996 and describe your home and we'll give you honest, upfront pricing over the phone.
