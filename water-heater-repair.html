FAQ

Water Heater Repair Questions

Quick answers. When in doubt, call 437-996-1996.

Do you repair both tank and tankless water heaters?

Yes. We repair, service, and install both conventional tank water heaters and tankless (on-demand) water heaters across the GTA, including gas and electric units. Call 437-996-1996 and describe your model and the issue for a fast diagnosis.

What should I do if my water heater is leaking?

Shut off the water supply to the tank and, if it's a gas unit, turn off the gas control valve. Then call 437-996-1996 right away — a leaking tank can fail completely and flood the area around it, so it's best treated as an urgent repair.

How much does water heater repair or replacement cost?

Cost depends on whether it's a repair or full replacement, and the type of unit. We give honest, upfront pricing over the phone at 437-996-1996 before any work begins, including options for tank and tankless replacements.

How fast can you respond to a no hot water call?

We prioritize no hot water calls and aim to get a local plumber out quickly across Toronto and the GTA. Call 437-996-1996 and we'll give you a clear timeframe based on your location.
