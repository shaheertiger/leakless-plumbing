Our Story

Trade Expertise, Modern Service

We started Leakless Plumbing because too many people in the GTA had the same story: a plumbing emergency, a phone that rang out, and hours of stress waiting for someone to call back. We wanted to do it differently.

Today, we combine genuine trade craftsmanship with the kind of responsive, upfront service you'd expect from any great local business. When you call, a real person answers. We explain what's happening, give you honest guidance and pricing over the phone, and get a qualified plumber to your door.

No confusing online forms. No waiting for an email. Just a straight line to a plumber who can help.
