Know the Warning Signs

Signs You Need Drain Cleaning

Drains rarely fail all at once — they warn you first. Spot these signs early and a quick call can save you from a messy backup later.

Slow Drainage

Water pooling in the sink, tub, or shower instead of draining away is the earliest sign a clog is forming. Clearing it now is far easier than after it fully blocks.

Gurgling Sounds

Gurgling from a drain or toilet means air is trapped behind a blockage. It is your plumbing telling you a clog is building somewhere in the line.

Bad Odours

Sewer-like smells rising from a drain point to trapped food, grease, or waste decaying inside the pipe. Cleaning the line clears both the clog and the smell.

Water Backing Up

Water rising in a sink when the washer runs, or in the tub when you flush, means a shared drain or the main line is blocked. This needs attention fast.

Multiple Slow Fixtures

When several drains slow down together, the problem is usually deeper in the main line rather than in any one fixture. Call before it becomes a full backup.

Recurring Clogs

A drain that keeps clogging after you plunge it has a deeper cause — grease buildup, roots, or a damaged pipe. A proper cleaning fixes the actual problem.

Sewer & Recurring Clogs

When It Is More Than a Simple Clog

Some drain problems keep coming back no matter how many times you plunge or pour cleaner down the line. That is almost always a sign the trouble is in the main sewer line — tree roots working their way into pipe joints, decades of grease and buildup narrowing the pipe, or a cracked or bellied section catching everything that flows past.

We clear main sewer line clogs across the GTA and use camera inspection to confirm the real condition of your pipe, so you are not paying to clear the same blockage twice.
