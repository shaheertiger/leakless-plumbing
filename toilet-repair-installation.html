FAQ

Toilet & Fixture Repair Questions

Quick answers. When in doubt, call 437-996-1996.

Do you fix running or constantly leaking toilets?

Yes. A running toilet or one leaking at the base usually points to a worn flapper, fill valve, or wax ring seal. Call 437-996-1996 and we'll diagnose and repair it, stopping the water waste and any leaking.

Can you install a new toilet, faucet, or sink?

Absolutely. We install new toilets, faucets, and sinks in homes and businesses across the GTA, with clean, leak-free connections. Call 437-996-1996 to discuss your fixtures and get a quote.

My toilet won't flush properly — is that a clog?

It could be a clog, a worn flush valve, or a blocked vent. Call 437-996-1996 and describe what's happening; we'll clear the blockage or repair the internal parts so it flushes properly again.

Do you repair shower and bathtub valves?

Yes, we repair and replace shower and bathtub valves for low pressure, leaks, or temperature control issues. Call 437-996-1996 for fast, licensed shower and tub plumbing service.
