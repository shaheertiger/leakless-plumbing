Local Knowledge

Plumbing That Fits North York's Housing

North York's plumbing work varies a lot depending on which part of the district you're in. Along Yonge Street through Willowdale and North York Centre, the housing is dominated by a dense corridor of high-rise and mid-rise condos, which means in-suite shut-off valves, shared stacks, and coordinating access with building management. A few blocks over, Bathurst Manor and Newtonbrook are full of established brick bungalows and side-splits, many still running original supply lines or drain piping that has narrowed with age. Closer to Bayview Village and Lawrence Park North, larger executive homes tend to have more elaborate fixture layouts, secondary bathrooms and finished basements that all need to be accounted for.

We work across all of it — condos, bungalows, side-splits and larger custom homes — and we diagnose before we recommend, so you are not paying to replace something that just needs a proper repair.
